Stori

What's Your Stori?

Share your ideas through pictures and voice. With Stori, you create slide shows on your mobile device and share them for viewing on the web or within the Stori app. Stori is available for iOS and Android.

Follow @WhatsYourStori

Your pictures. Your voice.

Everyone likes sharing photos. With Stori, you can record your voice with each picture. Create a slide show from the photos stored on your phone, recording your voice description for each picture.

Every Stori tells a picture

When your family or friends view your Stori, you are practically there, explaining in your recorded voice your thoughts, ideas, and experiences behind each photo. All together, your Stori describes your pictures.

Stori is easy to use

  1. Create a slide
  2. Add a photo from your phone, or use the camera to take a new shot
  3. Record your voice description
  4. Optionally add a text caption
  5. Repeat, adding additional slides

When you are finished creating, publish your Stori to the cloud, and share your Stori with anyone. Your recipients don't need to be running the Stori application. They can view it through their web browser - either on their phone, tablet, or desktop.
